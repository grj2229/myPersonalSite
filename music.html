Music

I began learning piano around the age of 5, and I've been studying classical piano ever since. Throughout my four years of college, I studied with Lois Shapiro. I also began taking voice lessons in sophomore year. I studied with Gale Fuller for one year and with Deborah Selig for two.

I performed a solo senior recital on May 1, 2022. It featured various piano works, all of which were based on songs, operas, or poems. I also had the chance to push myself out of my comfort zones and perform a few vocal works. In the years leading up to college, I had lost much of my self-confidence. However, my college musical journey not only rebuilt my confidence but also my love for playing and sharing music. I will be forever grateful to my professors, family, and friends who supported me throughout this time.
