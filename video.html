Video Editing

In college, I picked up video editing. What began as high school Chinese projects in GarageBand turned into seriously learning the Adobe Suite through free online resources.


As the Content Creator of the Wellesley Asian Dance Organization (WADO) during the pandemic, I not only danced but also created dance videos to simulate our members dancing together.


A few programs I learned through this experience were: Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Media Encoder.
