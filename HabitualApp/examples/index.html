Problem Statement

We found that students have difficulty finding motivation and maintaining their motivation to complete routines and build on their hobbies. This has only been exacerbated by the pandemic and social injustice worldwide. Our application allows users to create routines and to strive to have a healthier lifestyle.



Habitual's Solution

Modify routines or hobbies you want to pursue in a way that works best for you. Receive encouraging messages from friends, families and other people in your life.
